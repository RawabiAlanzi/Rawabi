CHANEL

History

Chanel is a French haute couture company, manufacturing ready-made garments, accessories, cosmetics and perfumes. The French designer Coco Chanel was founded in 1910. The company is now owned by Alan Fartemer and Gerard Wertheimer, grandson of Pierre Frantemer, co-founder of the group since 1924. Chanel is one of the most important international brands for which Forbes has not released the company's 2012 profit of approximately $ 1.6 billion. ]
